100k+ Steam Games Data, analysed and cleaned, performed EDA, finished up with a dashboard with a futuristic theme to it, respecting personal and professional preferences :)).

Business Requirements: 

KPI: Total Games
KPI: Total Estimated Owners
KPI: Average Price
KPI: Free Games Percentage
Trend: Games Released Per Year
Trend: Avg Estimated Owners Over Time
Trend: Avg Playtime by Release Year
Market Split: Platforms
Market Split: Price Composition
Market Split: Top Genres
Market Split: Top Categories
Top Performers: Most Players Online
Top Performers: Review Sentiment 

Tools Used: Excel, Python, Pandas, Numpy, Matplotlib, Seaborn, GoogleColab, PowerBI.

# This could have been done in MySQL (and I prefer it too imo), however, I chose python notebook to get more familiar with it :)).

Dataset: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/fronkongames/steam-games-dataset
